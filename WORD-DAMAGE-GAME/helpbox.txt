WELCOME TO THE HELP CONTENT OF CATCH PHRASE:

THE OBJECTIVE OF THIS GAME IS TO CREATE WORDS FROM THE MATRIX OF LETTERS 
AND REDUCE THE HEALTH OF THE OPPONENT TO ZERO.

YOU CAN FIGHT AGAINST THE COMPUTER OR AGAINST ANOTHER FRIEND FOR VICTORY.
EACH OF YOU WILL HAVE TO MAKE WORDS FROM A GRID OF LETTERS.
THE BETTER AND MORE COMLEX YOUR WORD THE MORE DAMAGE YOU INFLICT.

LET THE BEST PLAYER WIN!!
